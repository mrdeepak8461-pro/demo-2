Fiberglass casting

Woven fiberglass mesh impregnated with polyurethane, Mainly used as a substitute for plaster cast used for supporting broken bones. Activated by submersion in water. Easy to use, lightweight, yet durable.

  • 6in x 4yds (15cm x 3.6m)

  • Excellent lamination
  • Lightweight and easy mobility
