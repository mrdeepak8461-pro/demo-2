02. Info about the material:

We are offering gr cast cast tape, fiberglass cast, polyester cast, orthopedics, orthopedic cast . Gr cast® is the orthopedic fixed tape to make broken bones or damaged muscles cured by molding in the right position. It is made of 100% polyester and indurative resin , and it is 15 of the weight of plaster bandage, but has strength in the same level of existing glass-fiber products 25 times stronger than plaster bandage .

with the above features, it is an advanced cast which is much superior to an article of glass fiber in plasticity, moldability, safety, and durability. Also it is capable of satisfying customers’ interests by providing various patterns of fiber glass products.

PHIL-CAST is manufactured by combining POLYURETHANE , a hardener and FIBERGLASS with outstanding elasticity and flexibility. It is a product that becomes hardened through curing upon contact with water or moisture. Setting time for curing is within 5 minutes with full curing achieved within 20 minutes.

03. History

Four generations are working keenly for the development of Ayurveda. It was started by Shri Nand Singh ji , carried on by Hakim Hakim Rai ji who was a renowned Ayurvedic Physician practicing in Karol Bagh, Delhi and he used to treat large number of patients each day with Ayurveda medicine. His patients included Dr. Rajendra Prasad , Sh. V.V. Giri and other leading dignitaries of their times.

It is further carried on by Sh. Gian Chand Goomar ji , who has a sound knowledge of herbs and minerals and is an expert in diagnosis through pulse reading and providing treatment in Ayurveda.

Now presently its director is Sh. Karan Goomar who is carrying the responsibility of whole Kudos® Family.

Kudos® a G.M.P and I.S.O. Certified Manufacturing unit , is engaged in manufacturing and marketing of a wide range of Healthcare and Beauty care formulations. Our state of the art manufacturing units are located at Shimla in Himachal Pradesh and Faridabad, India.
